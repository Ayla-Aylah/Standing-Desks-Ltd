Frequently Asked Questions

How long-lasting are your standing desks, and what components are used for building them?

High-quality components like solid wood, metal frames, and strengthened steel systems go into the manufacturing of our standing desks. They last for many years of regular use because they are sustainable.

Do your standing desks accommodate both sitting and standing postures, and can you change their height?

Yes, you may easily switch between sitting and standing thanks to the height-adjustable nature of all our desks. For enhanced convenience, we provide both manual and electric adjustment options.

Are your desks protected by a guarantee of quality, and so, what is it??

Yes, we serve an assurance of five years that covers architectural resilience, electrical issues (for electric stations), and produce weaknesses.

Is it possible to customise the desk's measurements, colour, or extra features to suit my workstation?

Of course! To meet your office's requests, we provide a choice of sizes, colour actions, and peripherals including integrated electrical outlets, monitor arms, and management of wire.
